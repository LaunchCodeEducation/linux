## Name

> Cron - daemon to execute scheduled commands

## Purpose

Fire off a command at the exact time you wish and to repeat as often as you would like.
- Time interval options:
  - `minute`
  - `hour`
  - `day-of-month`
  - `month`
  - `day-of-week`
